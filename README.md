# AquaFlow AI

AquaFlow AI is an AI-powered fish pond monitoring and decision support platform designed for smart aquaculture management under the STEM for Girls initiative.

## Project Overview

The platform helps users:
- record daily fish pond data
- monitor feed usage, mortality, and water conditions
- generate AI-powered recommendations
- detect risky pond conditions
- trigger alerts automatically
- visualize pond performance through a dashboard
- automate notifications and weekly summaries using n8n

## Problem It Solves

Small-scale fish pond management is often manual, making it difficult to track pond health, feeding, mortality, and performance consistently. AquaFlow AI improves monitoring, supports decision-making, and helps reduce losses through AI and automation.
